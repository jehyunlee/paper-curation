## VISUAL SPECIFICATION (ABSOLUTE)

**Orientation:** Horizontal, left-to-right temporal flow (2021 → 2026)

**Structure:** Four parallel ribbon-streams, variable width per activity intensity, flowing left-to-right with temporal progression

**Canvas:** 16:9 aspect ratio, white background, 100% opacity

**Stream Representation:**
- Each stream as continuous ribbon
- Width modulates by research intensity
- Color differentiation by stream identity (no color names in labels)
- Interaction zones marked by overlapping ribbon areas

**Interaction Visualization:**
- FEED: Thin directional arrow, target stream side-margin
- MERGE: Overlapping ribbon envelope, extended contact zone
- SPAWN: Bifurcation point, origin stream tapers, new stream emerges
- ENABLE: Dashed arrow pathway between streams

**Milestones:**
- Vertical datum lines at event years
- Triangle markers: ▶ (emergence/growth), ◀ (decline/transition)
- Milestone labels positioned above/below ribbons, outside main flow

**Labels (all mandatory, external to ribbons):**
- Stream names, left margin, centered vertically per stream
- Sub-sub-theme names within ribbon zones or adjacent boxes
- Tool/framework names in italics, positioned near relevant sub-sub-theme
- Interaction labels on arrows/zones (FEED, MERGE, SPAWN, ENABLE)
- Year markers below timeline baseline

**Prohibited Elements:**
- Title text
- Watercolor, gradient, or soft-edge effects
- Paper count indicators
- Color name text (use visual differentiation only)
- Unlabeled branches
- External decorative elements

**Typography:** Sans-serif, high contrast, minimum 10pt readable size
