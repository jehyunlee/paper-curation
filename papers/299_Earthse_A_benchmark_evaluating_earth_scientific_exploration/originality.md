In this paper, we present a comprehensive and professional benchmark for the Earth sciences, designed to evaluate the capabilities of LLMs in scientific exploration within this domain, spanning from fundamen- tal to advanced levels.. Leveraging a corpus of 100,000 research papers, we first construct two Question Answering (QA) datasets: Earth-Iron, which offers exten- sive question coverage for broad assessment, and Earth-Silver, which features a higher level of difficulty to evaluate professional depth..
