Concretely, given two research ideas (e.g., two jailbreaking methods), we aim to predict which will perform better on a set of benchmarks.. We scrape ideas and experimental results from conference papers, yielding 1,585 human-verified idea pairs published after our base model’s cut-off date for testing, and 6,000 pairs for training.. We then develop a system that combines a fine-tuned GPT-4.1 with a paper retrieval agent, and we recruit 25 human experts to compare with..
