To enable the commu- nity to better use KGs, we introduce a new dataset, FACTKG: Fact Verification via Rea- soning on Knowledge Graphs.. It consists of 108k natural language claims with five types of reasoning: One-hop, Conjunction, Exis- tence, Multi-hop, and Negation..
