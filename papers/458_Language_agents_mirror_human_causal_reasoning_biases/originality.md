In this work, we examine LMs’ ability to explore and infer causal relationships, using the well-established Blicket Test paradigm from devel- opmental psychology.. We find that LMs reliably infer the common, intuitive disjunctive causal relationships but systematically struggle with the un- usual, yet equally (or sometimes even more) evidenced conjunctive ones..
