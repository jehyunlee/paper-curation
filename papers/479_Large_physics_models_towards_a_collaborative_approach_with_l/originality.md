Abstract This paper explores ideas and provides a potential roadmap for the de- velopment and evaluation of physics-specific large-scale AI models, which we refer to as Large Physics Models (LPMs).. These models, based on founda- tion models such as Large Language Models (LLMs) - trained on broad data - are tailored to address the unique demands of physics research.. LPMs can function independently or as part of an integrated framework..
