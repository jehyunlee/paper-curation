We report Evo, a genomic foundation model that enables prediction and generation tasks from the molecular to genome scale.. Using an architecture based on advances in deep signal processing, we scale Evo to 7 billion parameters with a context length of 131 kilobases (kb) at single- nucleotide, byte resolution.. Trained on 2.7M prokaryotic and phage genomes, Evo can generalize across the three fundamental modalities of the central dogma of molecular biology to perform zero-shot function prediction that is competitive with, or outperforms, leading domain-specific language models..
