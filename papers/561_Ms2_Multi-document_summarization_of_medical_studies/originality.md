This dataset facilitates the development of systems that can assess and aggregate contradictory ev- idence across multiple studies, and is the first large-scale, publicly available multi-document summarization dataset in the biomedical do- main.. We experiment with a summarization system based on BART, with promising early results, though significant work remains to achieve higher summarization quality.. We for- mulate our summarization inputs and targets in both free text and structured forms and mod- ify a recently proposed metric to assess the quality of our system’s generated summaries.
