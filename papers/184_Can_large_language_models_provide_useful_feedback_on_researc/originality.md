With the breakthrough of large language models (LLM) such as GPT-4, there is growing interest in using LLMs to generate scientific feedback on research manuscripts.. However, the utility of LLM-generated feedback has not been systematically studied.. To address this gap, we created an automated pipeline using GPT-4 to provide comments on the full PDFs of scientific papers.. We evaluated the quality of GPT-4’s feedback through two large-scale studies..
