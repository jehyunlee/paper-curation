Addressing this gap, we introduce ScholarChemQA, a large-scale QA dataset constructed from chemical papers.. Specifically, the questions are from paper titles with a question mark, and the multi-choice answers are reasoned out based on the corresponding abstracts.. We gathered a collection of up to 40k instances, from which we annotated 1,050 answer labels for training, validation, and testing.. This dataset reflects typical real-world challenges, including an imbalanced data distribution and a substantial amount of unlabeled data that can be potentially useful..
