Abstract: Objectives: To quantify and characterize the use of Real World Evidence (RWE) in the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid’s (CMS) in CMS drug pricing negotiations to assess the transparency and methodological consistency of RWE incorporation.. Methods: We extracted and reviewed all 1,822 unique citations across the ten MFP Explanation documents available on CMS’s website.. For each citation, we identified whether the study constituted RWE, the data source (e.g., claims, electronic health records registry/clinical cohort), study design, and whether external health technology assessments were cited.. We calculated drug-specific and overall RWE proportions and summarized the methodological features of RWE studies.. Results: RWE was cited for all ten drugs, but its use varied substantially..
