The benchmark includes 10 datasets that cover different types of table reasoning capabilities across varied do- mains.. ToRR goes beyond model performance rankings, and is designed to reflect whether models can handle tabular data consistently and robustly, across a variety of common table rep- resentation formats.. We present a leaderboard as well as comprehensive analyses of the re- sults of leading models over ToRR..
