Abstract Introduction.. This paper reports on an ongoing research project that investigates the development of research support services (RSS) in China’s university libraries to facilitate AI for science (AI4S) projects.. Specifically, this paper focuses on developing a conceptual model based on a literature review.. A systematic literature review approach was adopted.. English and Chinese academic databases were searched.. After screening, 67 articles were analysed using a thematic analysis and qualitative coding.. The analysis points to a conceptual model that consists of 21 library RSS and five main themes: promoting AI readiness, formulating research ideas, building collaborative networks, conducting research, and publishing results..
