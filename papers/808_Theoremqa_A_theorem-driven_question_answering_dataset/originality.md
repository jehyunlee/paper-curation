In this paper, we introduce TheoremQA, the first theorem-driven question-answering dataset de- signed to evaluate AI models’ capabilities to apply theorems to solve challenging science problems.. TheoremQA is curated by domain experts containing 800 high-quality questions covering 350 theorems1 from Math, Physics, EE&CS, and Finance.. We evaluate a wide spec- trum of 16 large language and code models with different prompting strategies like Chain-of- Thoughts and Program-of-Thoughts.. We found that GPT-4’s capabilities to solve these prob- lems are unparalleled, achieving an accuracy of 51% with Program-of-Thoughts Prompting.. All the existing open-sourced models are below 15%,
