In this work, we present BIODSA-1K, a benchmark designed to evaluate AI agents on realistic, data-driven biomedical hypothesis validation tasks.. BIODSA-1K consists of 1,029 hypothesis- centric tasks paired with 1,177 analysis plans, curated from over 300 published biomedical studies to reflect the structure and reasoning found in authentic re- search workflows.. Each task includes a structured hypothesis derived from the original study’s conclusions, expressed in the affirmative to reflect the language of scientific reporting, and one or more pieces of supporting evidence grounded in empirical data tables..
