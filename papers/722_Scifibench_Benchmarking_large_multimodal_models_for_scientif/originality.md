In this work, we present SciFIBench, a scientific figure interpretation benchmark consisting of 2000 questions split between two tasks across 8 categories.. The questions are curated from arXiv paper figures and captions, using adversarial filtering to find hard negatives and human verification for quality control.. We evaluate 28 LMMs on SciFIBench, finding it to be a challenging benchmark.. Finally, we investigate the alignment and reasoning faithfulness of the LMMs on augmented question sets from our benchmark.. We release SciFIBench to encourage progress in this domain: https://scifibench.github.io/.
