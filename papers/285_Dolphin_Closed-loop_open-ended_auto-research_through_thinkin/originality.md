To further move towards the ulti- mate goal (i.e., automatic scientific research), in this paper, we introduce DOLPHIN, a closed- loop LLM-driven framework to enhance the au- tomation level of scientific research.. DOLPHIN first generates novel ideas based on feedback from previous experiments and relevant papers ranked by the topic and task attributes.. Then, the generated ideas can be implemented us- ing a code template refined and debugged with the designed exception-traceback-guided local code structure.. Finally, DOLPHIN automati- cally analyzes the results of each idea and feeds the results back to the next round of idea gener- ation.
