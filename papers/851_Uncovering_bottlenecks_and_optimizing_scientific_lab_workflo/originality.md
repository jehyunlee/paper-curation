We introduce Cycle Time Reduction Agents (CTRA), a LangGraph-based agentic workflow designed to automate the analysis of lab operational metrics.. CTRA comprises three main components: the Question Creation Agent for initiating analysis, Operational Metrics Agents for data extraction and validation, and Insights Agents for reporting and visualization, identifying bottlenecks in lab processes.. This paper details CTRA’s architecture, evaluates its performance on a lab dataset, and discusses its potential to accelerate pharmaceutical and biotechnological development..
