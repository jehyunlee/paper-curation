We present WITH- DRARXIV, the first large-scale dataset of with- drawn papers from arXiv, containing over 14,000 papers and their associated retraction comments spanning the repository’s entire his- tory through September 2024.. Through care- ful analysis of author comments, we develop a comprehensive taxonomy of retraction rea- sons, identifying 10 distinct categories ranging from critical errors to policy violations.. We demonstrate a simple yet highly accurate zero- shot automatic categorization of retraction rea- sons, achieving a weighted average F1-score of 0.9594.. Additionally, we release1 WITH- DRARXIV-SCIFY, an enriched version includ- ing scripts for parsed full-text PDFs, specifi- cally designed to enable research in scientific feasibility studies, claim verification, and auto- mated theorem proving.
