To bridge this gap, we in- troduce DSBench, a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate data science agents with realistic tasks.. This benchmark includes 466 data analysis tasks and 74 data modeling tasks, sourced from ModelOff and Kaggle competitions.. DSBench offers a realistic setting by encompassing long contexts, multimodal task back- grounds, reasoning with large data files and multi-table structures, and performing end-to-end data modeling tasks.
