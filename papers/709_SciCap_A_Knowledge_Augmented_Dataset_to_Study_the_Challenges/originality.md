Unlike previous studies, we re- frame scientific figure captioning as a knowledge-augmented image captioning task that models need to utilize knowl- edge embedded across modalities for caption generation.. To this end, we extended the large-scale SciCap dataset (Hsu, Giles, and Huang 2021) to SciCap+ which includes mention- paragraphs (paragraphs mentioning figures) and OCR tokens.. Then, we conduct experiments with the M4C-Captioner (a multimodal transformer-based model with a pointer network) as a baseline for our study..
