In this paper, we present AI Idea Bench 2025, a framework designed to quantitatively evaluate and compare the ideas generated by LLMs within the domain of AI research from diverse perspectives.. The framework comprises a comprehensive dataset of 3,495 AI papers and their associated inspired works, along with a robust evaluation methodology.. This evaluation system gauges idea quality in two dimensions: align- ment with the ground-truth content of the original papers and judgment based on general reference material.
