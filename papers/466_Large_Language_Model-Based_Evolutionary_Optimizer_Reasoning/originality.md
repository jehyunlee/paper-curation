We introduce a novel population-based method for numerical optimization using LLMs called Language-Model-Based Evolution- ary Optimizer (LEO).. Our hypothesis is supported through numerical examples, spanning benchmark and industrial engineering problems such as supersonic nozzle shape optimiza- tion, heat transfer, and windfarm layout optimization.. We compare our method to several gradient-based and gradient-free optimization approaches.. While LLMs yield comparable results to state-of-the-art methods, their imaginative nature and propensity to hallucinate demand careful handling..
