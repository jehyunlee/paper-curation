Moro 2, 00185 Roma, Italy We study the distributions of citations received by a single publication within several disciplines, spanning broad areas of science.. We show that the probability that an article is cited c times has large variations between different disciplines, but all distributions are rescaled on a universal curve when the relative indicator cf = c/c0 is considered, where c0 is the average number of citations per article for the discipline..
