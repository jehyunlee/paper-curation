However, a prevalent issue is “superficial self-reflection”, where models fail to robustly verify their own outputs.. We introduce RISE (Reinforcing Reasoning with Self- Verification), a novel online RL framework designed to tackle this.. RISE explicitly and simultaneously trains an LLM to improve both its problem-solving and self-verification abilities within a single, integrated RL process.. The core mechanism involves leveraging verifiable rewards from an outcome verifier to provide on-the-fly feedback for both solution generation and self-verification tasks.. In each iteration, the model generates solutions, then critiques its own on-policy generated solutions, with both trajectories contributing to the policy update..
