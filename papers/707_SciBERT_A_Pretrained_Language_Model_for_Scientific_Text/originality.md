We release SCIBERT, a pretrained language model based on BERT (Devlin et al., 2019) to address the lack of high-quality, large-scale labeled scientific data.. SCIBERT leverages unsupervised pretraining on a large multi-domain corpus of scientific publications to improve perfor- mance on downstream scientific NLP tasks.. We evaluate on a suite of tasks including sequence tagging, sentence classification and dependency parsing, with datasets from a variety of scientific domains.. We demon- strate statistically significant improvements over BERT and achieve new state-of-the- art results on several of these tasks..
