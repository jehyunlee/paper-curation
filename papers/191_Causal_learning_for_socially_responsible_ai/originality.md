Abstract There have been increasing concerns about Ar- tificial Intelligence (AI) due to its unfathomable potential power.. To make AI address ethical challenges and shun undesirable outcomes, re- searchers proposed to develop socially responsible AI (SRAI).. One of these approaches is causal learn- ing (CL).. We survey state-of-the-art methods of CL for SRAI.. We begin by examining the seven CL tools to enhance the social responsibility of AI, then review how existing works have succeeded using these tools to tackle issues in developing SRAI such as fairness..
