Abstract This paper introduces Qatent PatFig, a novel large-scale patent figure dataset comprising 30,000+ patent figures from over 11,000 European patent applications.. For each figure, this dataset provides short and long captions, refer- ence numerals, their corresponding terms, and the minimal claim set that describes the interactions between the com- ponents of the image.. To assess the usability of the dataset, we finetune an LVLM model on Qatent PatFig to generate short and long descriptions, and we investigate the effects of incorporating various text-based cues at the prediction stage of the patent figure captioning process..
