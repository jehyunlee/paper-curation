Conclusion In this paper, we introduced an agent system capable of understanding and executing quantum chemistry tasks with a high degree of success.. We also demonstrate the agentic architecture’s ability to generalize to new problems that it has not been previously taught to do and successfully simulate a lanthanoid complex and an IR spectrum.. Furthermore, its ability to visualize the molecule and export the action traces allows for human expert inspection and expedites building workflows for virtual high-throughput screening.. These capabilities pave the way to more accessible and less time-consuming computational chemistry tasks..
