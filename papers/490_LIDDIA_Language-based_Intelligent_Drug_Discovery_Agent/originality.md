Recent advances in artificial intelligence for chemistry have sought to expedite individual drug dis- covery tasks; however, there remains a crit- ical need for an intelligent agent that can navigate the drug discovery process.. To- wards this end, we introduce LIDDIA, an au- tonomous agent capable of intelligently navi- gating the drug discovery process in silico.. By leveraging the reasoning capabilities of large language models, LIDDIA serves as a low- cost and highly-adaptable tool for autonomous drug discovery..
