In this work, we address measuring and enhancing the diversity of the text-to-SQL demonstration pool.. First, we introduce a diversity metric and present that the diversity of the existing label- ing data can be further enhanced.. Motivated by these findings, we propose FUSED that iter- atively fuses demonstrations to create a diverse demonstration pool based on human labeling or even from scratch with LLMs, reducing la- beling costs..
