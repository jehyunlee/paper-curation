We analyze the careers of 12,000 US faculty across 15 disciplines to reveal key patterns, pre- and post-tenure.. Publication rates rise sharply during the tenure-track, peaking just before tenure.. However, post-tenure trajectories diverge: Researchers in lab-based fields sustain high output, while those in non-lab-based fields typically exhibit a decline.. After tenure, faculty produce more novel works, though fewer highly cited papers..
