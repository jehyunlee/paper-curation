Abstract We introduce TLDR generation, a new form of extreme summarization, for scientific pa- pers.. TLDR generation involves high source compression and requires expert background knowledge and understanding of complex domain-specific language.. To facilitate study on this task, we introduce SCITLDR, a new multi-target dataset of 5.4K TLDRs over 3.2K papers.. SCITLDR contains both author-written and expert-derived TLDRs, where the latter are collected using a novel annotation pro- tocol that produces high-quality summaries while minimizing annotation burden.. We pro- pose CATTS, a simple yet effective learn- ing strategy for generating TLDRs that ex- ploits titles as an auxiliary training signal.. CATTS improves upon strong baselines un- der both automated metrics and human evalua- tions..
