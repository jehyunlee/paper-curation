However, to evaluate such systems, people either rely on direct Question-Answering (QA) to the LLM itself, or in a biomedical experimental manner.. How to precisely benchmark biomedical agents from an AI Scientist perspective remains largely unexplored.. To this end, we draw inspiration from one most important abilities of scientists, understanding the literature, and introduce BioKGBench..
