We present an automated review generation method based on large language models (LLMs) to overcome efficiency bottlenecks and reduce cognitive load.. Our statistically validated evaluation framework demonstrates that the generated reviews match or exceed manual quality, offering broad applicability across research fields without requiring users’ domain knowledge.. Applied to propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts, our method swiftly analyzed 343 articles, averaging seconds per arti- cle per LLM account, producing comprehensive reviews spanning 35 topics, with extended analysis of 1041 articles providing insights into catalysts’ properties..
