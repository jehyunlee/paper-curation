Abstract We present phi-4, a 14-billion parameter language model developed with a training recipe that is centrally focused on data quality.. Unlike most language models, where pre-training is based pri- marily on organic data sources such as web content or code, phi-4 strategically incorporates synthetic data throughout the training process.. While previous models in the Phi family largely distill the capabilities of a teacher model (specifically GPT-4), phi-4 substantially surpasses its teacher model on STEM-focused QA capabilities, giving evidence that our data-generation and post-training tech- niques go beyond distillation..
