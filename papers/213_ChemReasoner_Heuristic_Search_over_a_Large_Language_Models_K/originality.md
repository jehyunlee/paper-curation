Identified catalysts in interme- diate search steps undergo structural evaluation based on spatial orientation, reaction pathways, and stability.. Scoring functions based on adsorp- tion energies and reaction energy barriers steer the exploration in the LLM’s knowledge space to- ward energetically favorable, high-efficiency cat- alysts..
