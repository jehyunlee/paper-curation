In this paper, we develop a neural data-driven summarizer by leverag- ing the seq2seq paradigm, in which a joint context-driven attention mechanism is pro- posed to measure the contextual relevance within full texts and a heterogeneous bibliog- raphy graph simultaneously.. Our motivation is to maintain the topic coherency between a related work section and its target document, where both the textual and graphic contexts play a big role in characterizing the relation- ship among scientific publications accurately..
