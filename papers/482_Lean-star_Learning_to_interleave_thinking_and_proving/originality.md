We present Lean-STaR, a framework for training language models to produce informal thoughts prior to each step of a proof, thereby boosting the model’s theorem-proving capabilities.. Lean-STaR uses retrospective ground-truth tactics to generate synthetic thoughts for training the language model.. At inference time, the trained model directly generates the thoughts prior to the prediction of the tactics in each proof step..
