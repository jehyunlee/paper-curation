We propose mephisto, a multi-agent collaboration framework that mimics human reasoning to interpret multi-band galaxy observations.. mephisto interacts with the CIGALE codebase, which includes spectral energy distribution (SED) models to explain observations.. In this open-world setting, mephisto learns from its self-play experience, performs tree search, and accumulates knowledge in a dynamically updated base.. As a proof of concept, we apply mephisto to the latest data from the James Webb Space Telescope.. mephisto attains near-human proficiency in reasoning about galaxies’ physical scenarios, even when dealing with a recently discovered population of "Little Red Dot" galaxies..
