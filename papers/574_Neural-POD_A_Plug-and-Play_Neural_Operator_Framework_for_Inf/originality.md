We introduce Neural Proper Orthogonal Decomposition (Neural-POD), a plug-and-play neural operator that learns nonlinear, orthogonal basis functions directly in function space and can be inte- grated in both projection-based reduced order models and operator-learning frameworks such as DeepONet.. Neural-POD replaces SVD-derived, resolution- dependent linear modes with continuous, resolution-invariant bases learned via sequential residual minimization, analogous to Gram–Schmidt orthogonaliza- tion.. The framework supports training under task-specific norms (e.g., L2, L1), improves out-of-distribution generalization to unseen parameter regimes, and captures nonlinear structure in complex systems..
