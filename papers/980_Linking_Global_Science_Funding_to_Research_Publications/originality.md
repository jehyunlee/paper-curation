We present a dataset linking global science funding organizations to re- search publications by systematically disambiguating 7.4 million unique funding acknowledgment strings extracted from publication metadata.. Funder names are matched to standardized organizational identifiers using a multi-stage pipeline that combines lexical normalization, similarity- based clustering, rule-based matching, named entity recognition assis- tance, and manual validation.. The resulting dataset links 1.9 million unique funder strings to canonical organization identifiers and records match types and unresolved cases to support transparency.. Tech- nical validation includes paper-level comparisons across bibliometric sources and manual verification against full-text acknowledgment sec- tions, with reported recall and precision metrics..
