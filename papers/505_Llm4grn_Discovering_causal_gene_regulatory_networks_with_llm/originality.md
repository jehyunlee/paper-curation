In this work, we investigate the potential of large language models (LLMs) for GRN discovery, leveraging their learned biolog- ical knowledge alone or in combination with traditional statistical methods.. We develop a task-based evaluation strategy to address the challenge of unavailable ground truth causal graphs.. Specifically, we use the GRNs suggested by LLMs to guide causal synthetic data generation and compare the resulting data against the original dataset..
