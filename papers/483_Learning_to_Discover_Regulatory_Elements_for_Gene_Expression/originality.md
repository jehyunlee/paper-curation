Here, we introduce Seq2Exp, a Sequence to Expression network explicitly designed to discover and extract regulatory elements that drive target gene expression, enhancing the accuracy of the gene expression prediction.. Our approach captures the causal relationship between epigenomic signals, DNA se- quences and their associated regulatory elements.. Specifically, we propose to de- compose the epigenomic signals and the DNA sequence conditioned on the causal active regulatory elements, and apply an information bottleneck with the Beta dis- tribution to combine their effects while filtering out non-causal components..
