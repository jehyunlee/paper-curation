On top of this, we introduce InterFeedback-Bench that evaluates interactive intelligence using two representative datasets, MMMU-Pro and MathVerse, to test 10 dif- ferent open-source LMMs.. Additionally, we present InterFeedback-Human, a newly col- lected dataset of 120 cases designed for manu- ally testing interactive performance in propri- etary models such as OpenAI-o1 and Claude- Sonnet-4.. Our evaluation results indicate that even the state-of-the-art LMM, OpenAI-o1, struggles to refine its responses based on hu- man feedback, achieving an average score of less than 50%..
