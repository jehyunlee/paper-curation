In this work, we propose a novel agentic AI-based knowledge discovery-oriented virtual study group that aims to extract meaningful ageing- related biological knowledge considering highly ageing-related Gene Ontology terms that are selected by hierarchical feature selection methods.. We investigate the performance of the proposed agentic AI framework by considering four different model organisms’ ageing-related Gene Ontology terms and validate the biological findings by reviewing existing research articles..
