We present GraphMetaMat, a graph-based framework capable of designing three- dimensional metamaterials with programmable responses and arbitrary manufacturing constraints.. Integrating graph networks, physics biases, reinforcement learning, and tree search, GraphMetaMat can target stress-strain curves spanning four orders of magnitude and complex behaviors, as well as viscoelastic transmission responses with varying attenuation gaps..
