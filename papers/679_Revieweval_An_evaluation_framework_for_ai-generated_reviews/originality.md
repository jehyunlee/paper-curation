Abstract The escalating volume of academic research, coupled with a shortage of qualified review- ers, necessitates innovative approaches to peer review.. In this work, we propose: ❶Review- Eval, a comprehensive evaluation framework for AI-generated reviews that measures align- ment with human assessments, verifies factual accuracy, assesses analytical depth, identifies degree of constructiveness and adherence to reviewer guidelines; and ❷ReviewAgent, an LLM-based review generation agent featuring a novel alignment mechanism to tailor feedback to target conferences and journals, along with a self-refinement loop that iteratively optimizes its intermediate outputs and an external im- provement loop using ReviewEval to improve upon the final reviews.. ReviewAgent improves actionable insights by 6.78% and 47.62% over existing AI baselines and expert reviews re- spectively..
