In this paper, we present a comprehensive survey on recent advances in long-context modeling for large language models.. Our survey is structured around three key aspects: how to obtain effective and efficient LCLMs, how to train and deploy LCLMs efficiently, and how to evaluate and analyze LCLMs comprehensively.. For the first aspect, we discuss data strategies, architectural designs, and workflow approaches oriented with long context processing.. For the second aspect, we provide a detailed examina- tion of the infrastructure required for LCLM training and inference..
