Here, we present LARC, the first LLM-based Agentic framework for Retrosynthesis planning under Constraints.. LARC incorporates agen- tic constraint evaluation, through an Agent-as-a-Judge, directly into the retrosynthesis planning process, using agentic feedback grounded in tool- based reasoning to guide and constrain route generation..
