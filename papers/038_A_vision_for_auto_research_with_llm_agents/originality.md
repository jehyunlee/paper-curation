Abstract—This paper introduces Agent-Based Auto Research, a structured multi-agent framework designed to automate, co- ordinate, and optimize the full lifecycle of scientific research.. Leveraging the capabilities of large language models (LLMs) and modular agent collaboration, the system spans all major research phases, including literature review, ideation, methodology plan- ning, experimentation, paper writing, peer review response, and dissemination.. By addressing issues such as fragmented work- flows, uneven methodological expertise, and cognitive overload, the framework offers a systematic and scalable approach to scien- tific inquiry.. Preliminary explorations demonstrate the feasibility and potential of Auto Research as a promising paradigm for self- improving, AI-driven research processes..
