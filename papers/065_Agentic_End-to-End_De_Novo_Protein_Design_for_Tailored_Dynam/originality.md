Here, we introduce VibeGen, a generative AI framework that enables end-to-end de novo protein design conditioned on normal mode vibrations.. VibeGen employs an agentic dual- model architecture, comprising a protein designer that generates sequence candidates based on specified vibrational modes and a protein predictor that evaluates their dynamic accuracy.. This approach synergizes diversity, accuracy, and novelty during the design process..
