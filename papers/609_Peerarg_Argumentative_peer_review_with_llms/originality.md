In this paper, we propose a novel pipeline to support and understand the reviewing and decision- making processes of peer review: the PeerArg system combin- ing LLMs with methods from knowledge representation.. PeerArg takes in input a set of reviews for a paper and outputs the paper ac- ceptance prediction.. We evaluate the performance of the PeerArg pipeline on three different datasets, in comparison with a novel end-2-end LLM that uses few-shot learning to predict paper ac- ceptance given reviews..
