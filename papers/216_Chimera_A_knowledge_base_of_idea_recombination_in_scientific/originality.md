In this work, we introduce CHIMERA, a large-scale Knowledge Base (KB) of over 28K recombination examples automatically mined from the scientific literature.. CHIMERA enables large-scale empirical analysis of how scien- tists recombine concepts and draw inspiration from different areas, and enables training mod- els that propose novel, cross-disciplinary re- search directions.. To construct this KB, we define a new information extraction task: iden- tifying recombination instances in scientific abstracts.. We curate a high-quality, expert- annotated dataset and use it to fine-tune a large language model, which we apply to a broad corpus of AI papers..
