In this work, we propose a hybrid human-AI framework, MedSyn, where physicians and LLMs engage in multi-step, in- teractive dialogues to refine diagnoses and treatment decisions.. Unlike static decision-support tools, MedSyn enables dynamic exchanges, allowing physicians to challenge LLM suggestions while the LLM highlights alternative perspectives.. Through simulated physician-LLM inter- actions, we assess the potential of open-source LLMs as physician assistants..
