In this work, we introduce ScholarCopilot, a unified framework designed to enhance existing large language models for generating professional academic articles with accurate and contextu- ally relevant citations.. ScholarCopilot dynamically determines when to retrieve scholarly references by generating a retrieval token [RET], which is then used to query a citation database.. The retrieved references are fed into the model to augment the generation process.. We jointly optimize both the generation and citation tasks within a single framework to im- prove efficiency..
