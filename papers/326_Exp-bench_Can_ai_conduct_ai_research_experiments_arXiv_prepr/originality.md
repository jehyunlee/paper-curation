We introduce EXP-Bench, a novel benchmark designed to systematically evaluate AI agents on complete research experiments sourced from influential AI publications.. Given a research question and incomplete starter code, EXP-Bench challenges AI agents to formulate hypotheses, design and im- plement experimental procedures, execute them, and analyze results.. To enable the creation of such intricate and authentic tasks with high-fidelity, we design a semi-autonomous pipeline to extract and structure crucial experimental details from these research papers and their associated open-source code.. With the pipeline, EXP-Bench curated 461 AI research tasks from 51 top-tier AI research papers..
