Abstract We propose the first multi-agent framework for computational fluid dynamics that enables fully automated, end-to-end simulations directly from natural-language queries.. The approach integrates four specialized agents—Pre-processing, Prompt Generation, OpenFOAMGPT (simulator), and Post-processing—decomposing complex computational fluid dynamics workflows into collaborative components powered by large language models.. Extensive validation through diverse case stud- ies, including Poiseuille flows, single- and multi-phase porous media flows, and aerodynamic analyses, demonstrates 100% success and reproducibility rates across over 450 simulations.. Rigorous trustworthiness verification confirms that prop- erly designed multi-agent systems can achieve the reliability standards necessary for zero-tolerance scientific computing applications while significantly lowering entry barriers..
