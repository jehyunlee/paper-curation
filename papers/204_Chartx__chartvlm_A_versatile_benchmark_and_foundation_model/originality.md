In this paper, to comprehensively and rigor- ously benchmark the ability of the off-the-shelf MLLMs in the chart domain, we construct ChartX, a multi-modal evaluation set covering 18 chart types, 7 chart tasks, 22 disciplinary topics, and high-quality chart data.. Besides, we develop ChartVLM to offer a new perspective on handling multi-modal tasks that strongly depend on interpretable patterns, such as reasoning tasks in the field of charts or geometric images.. We evaluate the chart-related abil- ity of mainstream MLLMs and our ChartVLM on the proposed ChartX eval- uation set..
