In this paper, we reformulate the peer-review process as a multi-turn, long-context dialogue, incorporating distinct roles for authors, reviewers, and decision makers.. We construct a comprehensive dataset containing over 26,841 papers with 92,017 reviews collected from multiple sources, including the top-tier conference and prestigious journal.. This dataset is meticulously designed to facilitate the applications of LLMs for multi-turn di- alogues, effectively simulating the complete peer-review process..
