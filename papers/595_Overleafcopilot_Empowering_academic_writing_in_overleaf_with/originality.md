In this technical report, we explore the integration of LLMs and the popu- lar academic writing tool, Overleaf, to enhance the efficiency and quality of academic writing.. To achieve the above goal, there are three challenges: i) including seamless interaction between Overleaf and LLMs, ii) establishing reliable communication with the LLM provider, and iii) ensuring user privacy.. To address these challenges, we present OverleafCopilot, the first-ever tool (i.e., a browser ex- tension) that seamlessly integrates LLMs and Overleaf, enabling researchers to leverage the power of LLMs while writing papers.. Specifically, we first propose an effective framework to bridge LLMs and Overleaf..
