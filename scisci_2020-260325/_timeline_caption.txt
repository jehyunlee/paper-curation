### THEME 1: EQUITY & DIVERSITY IN SCIENCE
**Timeline: 2020-2026 | Papers: 12 | Status: DIVERGING & ACCELERATING**

**Origin (2020):** 
- Foundational work on diversity-innovation paradox and gender inequality in scientific careers
- Initial focus: demographic representation (gender, race) and career outcomes
- 2 papers establishing baseline measurements

**Development (2021-2022):**
- **2021**: Intersectionality framework introduced (Intersectional inequalities in science)
- Gender-diverse teams study expanded to 6.6M medicine papers
- **Divergence point**: Research splits into three sub-streams:
  - *Sub-stream A (Gender focus)*: Gender inequality, team composition effects
  - *Sub-stream B (Intersectionality)*: Race-gender-field interactions
  - *Sub-stream C (Career trajectories)*: Tenure effects, early-career setbacks

**Recent Evolution (2023-2026):**
- **2023**: Tenure and research trajectories (12,000+ professors, 15 fields)
- **2025**: Early career setback study extends to sports domain (novel domain transfer)
- **2026**: Authorship patterns in orthopedic surgery (field-specific application)
- Stream width: ACCELERATING (5 papers in 2023-2026 vs 3 in 2020-2022)

**Key Branching:**
- Gender → Intersectionality → Interdisciplinary effects
- Career stage → Tenure → Early-career setback → Long-term achievement

### THEME 3: SCIENTIFIC IMPACT & DISRUPTION MEASUREMENT
**Timeline: 2020-2026 | Papers: 13 | Status: DIVERGING & EVOLVING**

**Origin (2020):**
- Citation inequality analysis (global scale, 400M+ authors)
- Normalized inflation-corrected citation scores (nics metric)
- Focus: Concentration of citations, inequality trends

**Development (2021-2023):**
- **2021**: Global citation inequality rising (Gini coefficient 0.65→0.70)
- **2023**: Papers and patents becoming less disruptive (60M papers, 3.9M patents)
- **Divergence point**: Two competing frameworks emerge:
  - *Sub-stream A (Disruption)*: CD index, novelty metrics, breakthrough prediction
  - *Sub-stream B (Public impact)*: Policy use, news coverage, patent citations
  - *Sub-stream C (Social attention)*: Altmetrics, Twitter, public engagement

**Recent Evolution (2024-2026):**
- **2024**: Predicting breakthroughs via citation cascade dynamics (von Neumann entropy)
- **2025**: Disruptive papers vs. technology/society impact (paradox finding)
- **2025**: Novel papers and social attention (7 altmetric dimensions)
- **2026**: Real-world evidence in drug pricing (policy impact measurement)
- **2026**: Information pathways in online science communication

**Key Divergence:**
- Citation disruption → Novelty → Social attention (separate measurement streams)
- Academic impact ≠ Public impact (fundamental distinction)
- Stream width: ACCELERATING (6 papers in 2024-2026 vs 4 in 2020-2023)

**Paradigm Shift (2025):**
- Recognition that disruption ≠ societal impact (counterintuitive finding)
- Shift from single metrics to multi-dimensional impact assessment
